## 🗂️ Portfolio Structure

This repository is organized into the following categories to demonstrate different areas of my engineering expertise:

* 📁 **[01-Parts](./01-Parts)**
  * Individual 3D modeled components demonstrating proficiency in sketching, surfacing, and complex geometry creation.
* 📁 **[02-Assemblies](./02-Assemblies)**
  * Multi-part mechanical assemblies showcasing mating configurations, kinematics, and top-down/bottom-up design methodologies.
* 📁 **[03-Drawings](./03-Drawings)**
  * 2D engineering drafts and technical drawings featuring GD&T (Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing), bill of materials (BOM), and manufacturing specifications.
* 📁 **[04-FEA-Reports](./04-FEA-Reports)**
  * Finite Element Analysis (FEA) projects including static, dynamic, and thermal simulations. Contains setup details, mesh generation, boundary conditions, and PDF results.
* 📁 **[05-Optimization](./05-Optimization)**
  * Topology optimization and generative design case studies focused on weight reduction while maintaining structural integrity.
* 📁 **[06-Advanced-Projects](./06-Advanced-Projects)**
  * Capstone level work, full-scale machine designs, and comprehensive engineering challenges from initial concept to finalized simulation.
